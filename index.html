It's a Cole world, and I am trying to be the new Nat King

This webpage is the ground zero for all things Cole Johnson. The vision encircles written works, musical manuscripts, conversation worthy of contreversy, and hopefully so much more. My hope is that this page serves as a portfolio, an amalgimation if you will, of creativity, thought, music, passion, and intrigue. I expect this is not the beginning of the journey, but a means to record all that is to come. A webpage representing my passions, the blood-soaked gloves of an MMA bout, the lyrical genius of Nas or Jermaine, the divide between us of famine and fame, or how throughout Highschool all the jocks called me lame. This life is a pen in my hand lets make this site be the book.
